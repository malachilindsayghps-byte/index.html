About the Trinbarjam Elections Commission

Our Mission

The Trinbarjam Elections Commission is an independent body established to organize, supervise, and conduct all elections in Trinbarjam. Our primary mission is to ensure that elections are free, fair, transparent, and conducted in accordance with democratic principles and the laws of Trinbarjam.

Our History

Established in 1985 following the democratic reforms, the Commission has played a pivotal role in strengthening democracy in Trinbarjam. Over the years, we have successfully conducted 10 general elections and numerous local elections, earning recognition for our commitment to electoral integrity.

Our Values

  • Integrity: We maintain the highest ethical standards in all our operations.
  • Transparency: We ensure that all electoral processes are open to public scrutiny.
  • Impartiality: We treat all political parties and candidates equally.
  • Accountability: We take responsibility for our actions and decisions.
  • Efficiency: We strive to deliver excellent service with optimal use of resources.

Our Vision

To be a world-class electoral management body that strengthens democracy through credible, inclusive, and transparent elections, fostering public trust in the electoral process and contributing to the consolidation of democratic governance in Trinbarjam.

Key Functions

The Commission performs several critical functions to ensure the integrity of the electoral process:

  • Voter registration and maintenance of electoral rolls
  • Delimitation of constituencies
  • Registration of political parties
  • Monitoring election campaigns and expenditures
  • Conducting polling and counting of votes
  • Declaring election results
  • Voter education and awareness programs

Our Team

The Commission is composed of a Chairperson and four Commissioners appointed through a transparent process. Our dedicated staff of electoral professionals work tirelessly to ensure that every election meets the highest standards of fairness and transparency.
